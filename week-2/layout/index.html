New York

New York is the city where I have lived the longest. I enjoy many things about it and I know I will go back eventually. I moved there in 2002 and I left to come to Shanghai in 2015.

I lived in Brooklyn for the longest time, but I have also lived in Manhattan and Queens. Right before I left to come to China I was living in Long Island City, which is a neighborhood in the borough of Queens. Before that, I lived in Carroll Gardens, Brooklyn, which is a neighborhood known for great Italian restaurants.
